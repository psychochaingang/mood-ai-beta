Privacy Policy

GlitchAura Studios — Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GlitchAura Studios ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our apps and websites ("the Services").

1. Information We Collect

Camera Data: Apps that use camera access process all facial data locally on your device. No images, videos, or biometric data are ever recorded, stored, uploaded, or transmitted.

Usage Data: Some apps store preferences and usage history locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless explicitly stated.

No Account Required: Our apps and websites do not require account creation or login unless specified.

2. How We Use Information

All processing is done locally on your device for the sole purpose of detecting your mood and providing personalized joke responses. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

3. Data Storage

Calibration data and mood history are stored in local files on your device. You can delete these files anytime to reset the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may display ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymized data as per Google's Privacy Policy. The App itself does not share any user data with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

The App has a built-in age gate. Users under 16 are placed in Kid Mode with age-appropriate content. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App or deleting the local data files. The App does not store data on any server.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
